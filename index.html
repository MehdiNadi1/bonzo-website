THE BONZO WAY

Located in the heart of Essaouira, Morocco, Bonzo Coffee Shop fuses premium roasted coffee with top-tier comfort food like our famous Block Burger. Whether you're craving a rich espresso, a savory Double Grilled Cheese, or a sweet Bubble Waffle, our black and green aesthetic mirrors our approach: bold, modern, and electrifying.

OUR MENU

Block Burger $$

Our signature burger. A juicy, perfectly seared patty served with fresh ingredients and our secret neon sauce.

Chicken Sandwich $$

Void Espresso $$
